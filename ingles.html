Informative section:

What is?

Axupplies is a product management system whose objective is to help all small companies or that are just beginning to have a correct and efficient management of the products that they keep within their warehouses.

What Axupplies can do?

You can save records of products entered into the company's warehouses, keep track of the date to indicate the expiration of any product and if necessary, give the ability to manage employees, suppliers, users and the brands with which you have an agreement.

How it works?

It works according to records and access levels, this means that depending on the user's access level it is determined what he can or cannot do, what information he can or cannot see, and if he can make records or not. Essentially, this program takes the information given, encrypts what is necessary and saves it in the database, from which it then extracts the necessary information, decrypts what is necessary and puts it on display for the user.
